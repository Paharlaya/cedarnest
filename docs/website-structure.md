# CedarNest.com Website Structure Plan

## Based on Company Profile Analysis

### Core Brand Identity
- **Company**: Cedar Nest Web Studio
- **Tagline**: Building Intelligent Digital Ecosystems with Salesforce & AI
- **Primary Services**: Salesforce Solutions, AI Agentforce, Web Development
- **Target Audience**: Enterprise businesses seeking Salesforce transformation

## Proposed Website Sections

### 1. Hero Section
- Company logo and tagline
- Primary value proposition
- Call-to-action for consultation
- Visual: Professional tech/AI aesthetic

### 2. Core Services (3 Main Pillars)
#### A. Salesforce Solutions
- Sales Cloud
- Service Cloud
- Experience Cloud
- Integration & Architecture

#### B. Salesforce Agentforce & AI
- AI Service Agents
- AI Sales Agents
- Custom AI Development

#### C. Web Development & Digital Solutions
- Custom Development
- UI/UX Design
- Technical Development

### 3. Our Approach (4-Step Process)
- Strategy First
- Architecture & Planning
- Agile Implementation
- Optimization & Monitoring

### 4. Why Choose Cedar Nest
- Key differentiators
- Security-first approach
- Proven expertise

### 5. Mission & Values
- Company mission
- Core values and principles

### 6. Contact & Consultation
- Contact form
- Consultation booking
- Contact information

## Design Principles
- **Professional & Technical**: Clean, modern design reflecting enterprise focus
- **Trust & Security**: Emphasis on security-first approach
- **Scalable & Intelligent**: Visual elements suggesting growth and AI
- **Blue & White Color Scheme**: Professional, trustworthy (matching logo)
- **Clear Navigation**: Easy access to technical expertise areas

## Content Strategy
- Focus on business outcomes, not just features
- Emphasize measurable improvements and ROI
- Technical credibility without overwhelming jargon
- Case studies and proof points (when available)
- Clear calls-to-action for enterprise decision makers
